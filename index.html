My Previous Project



GiveCommit

GiveCommit is a mobile application designed to facilitate charitable donations by connecting donors with organizations that accept various types of contributions.

Utilizing Firebase for database management, user login and signup, and storage, the app aims to provide a seamless and secure donation experience. The application is built to serve three types of users: donors, organizations, and administrators, each with distinct functionalities to ensure an efficient donation process.

For the completion of UPLB Institute of Computer Science’s CMSC 23: Mobile Computing course, GiveCommit’s user interface was designed by myself and the app was developed by Dave Elcarte, Julius Namata and me in May 2024 using Dart and Flutter.
