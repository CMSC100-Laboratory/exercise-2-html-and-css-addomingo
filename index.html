GiveCommit

GiveCommit is a mobile application designed to facilitate charitable donations by connecting donors with organizations that accept various types of contributions.

Utilizing Firebase for database management, user login and signup, and storage, the app aims to provide a seamless and secure donation experience. The application is built to serve three types of users: donors, organizations, and administrators, each with distinct functionalities to ensure an efficient donation process.

For the completion of UPLB Institute of Computer Science’s CMSC 23: Mobile Computing course, GiveCommit’s user interface was designed by myself and the app was developed by Dave Elcarte, Julius Namata and me in May 2024 using Dart and Flutter.

About Me

Arianna Judelle D. Domingo was born on May 26, 2004, and was raised in her hometown located in Bacoor, Cavite. Throughout her childhood, she mostly focused on her academics and had few hobbies.

This cycle of monotony was disrupted by the end of elementary school, when her friend introduced her to the world of art. Since then, her hobbies revolved around art and handicrafts.

Originally supposed to take up a degree program much more related to art, such as architecture, Arianna eventually chose to take BS Computer Science in the University of the Philippines Los Baños. This was because she was in awe by the interface of a certain website, which inspired her to strive to make visually appealing websites of her own aesthetic.

Arianna loves the color Blue, and tends to favor minimalistic and elegant designs. However, she is always eager to broaden her area of creativity and explore other varieties of design.
